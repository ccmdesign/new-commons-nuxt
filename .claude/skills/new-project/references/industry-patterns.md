# Industry Patterns

This document provides industry-specific rules, compliance requirements, and content patterns.

## Legal Industry (Brazil)

### OAB Compliance (Provimento 205/2021)

Brazilian Bar Association (OAB) regulates lawyer advertising. These rules are MANDATORY for legal content.

#### Prohibited Phrases

| Category | Prohibited Examples |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Result promises** | "Causa ganha", "Sucesso garantido", "Garanta sua vitória", "100% de aproveitamento" |
| **Aggressive language** | "Contrate agora!", "Ligue já!", "Não perca tempo!", "Aproveite!" |
| **Pricing/discounts** | "Consulta grátis", "Honorários acessíveis", "Desconto", "Promoção", "Preço especial" |
| **Superlatives** | "A melhor advogada", "Número 1 em...", "Líder em...", "Incomparável", "Imbatível" |
| **Commercial commands** | "Contrate", "Compre", "Adquira", "Invista" |
| **Comparisons** | "Melhor que outros escritórios", "Mais experiente que..." |

#### Permitted Language

| Style | Example |
|-------|---------|
| **Welcoming** | "Se você está nessa situação, posso ajudar." |
| **Educational** | "Quer entender melhor? Me manda uma mensagem." |
| **Neutral WhatsApp** | "Fala comigo pelo WhatsApp: (XX) XXXXX-XXXX" |
| **Consultative** | "Cada caso é diferente. Se quiser conversar sobre o seu, estou à disposição." |
| **Informative** | "Se tiver dúvidas sobre sua situação específica, podemos conversar." |

#### CTA Validation Checklist

Before publishing any legal content CTA:

- [ ] No result promises?
- [ ] No aggressive/urgent language?
- [ ] No pricing mentions?
- [ ] No superlatives?
- [ ] No commercial commands?
- [ ] WhatsApp contact included with warm tone?
- [ ] OAB-compliant language?

### Legal Industry Voice Patterns

**Voice Archetype:** "Advogada Acessível e Educadora" (Accessible Lawyer & Educator)

**Key Traits:**
- Translates legalese into plain language
- Uses real cases (anonymized) to illustrate
- Validates reader concerns and fears
- Focuses on prevention and education
- Warm but professional tone

**Content Length:** 800-1,500 words (shorter for accessibility)

**Platform Focus:** LinkedIn + Instagram

**Topic Categories (Real Estate Law):**
- `regularizacao` - Property regularization
- `contratos` - Real estate contracts
- `inventario` - Probate, inheritance
- `usucapiao` - Adverse possession
- `familia` - Family property transfers
- `compra-segura` - Safe purchase analysis

## Design/Creative Industry

### Voice Archetype

**Voice:** "Confident, Clear-Sighted Guide"

**Key Traits:**
- Authoritative expertise without arrogance
- Translates complexity into clarity
- Purpose-driven, impact-focused
- Thought leadership positioning
- Professional empathy

### Content Patterns

**Content Length:** 1,200-2,000 words (more detail expected)

**Platform Focus:** LinkedIn + X (Twitter) + Instagram

**Topic Categories:**
- `publications` - Publication design, reports
- `data-viz` - Data visualization
- `brand` - Brand systems, identity
- `digital` - Digital platforms, interactive
- `strategy` - Design strategy, planning

### CTA Style

More consultative and process-focused:

```
"If you're facing similar challenges with your annual report design, let's discuss how we can help."

"Ready to transform your research into engaging digital content? Schedule a consultation."
```

## Healthcare Industry

### Compliance Considerations

Healthcare marketing has specific regulations that vary by country.

**Brazil (CFM/CRM):**
- No result guarantees
- No before/after without proper consent
- No pricing in public advertising
- Professional credentials must be displayed

**General Guidelines:**
- Focus on patient education
- Avoid medical advice in content
- Include appropriate disclaimers
- Emphasize care and compassion

### Voice Archetype

**Voice:** "Caring Expert" or "Trusted Health Guide"

**Key Traits:**
- Compassionate and reassuring
- Educational without being condescending
- Clear about limitations
- Focuses on patient empowerment

**Content Length:** 600-1,200 words (accessible)

**Platform Focus:** LinkedIn + Instagram

## Platform-Specific Patterns

### LinkedIn (All Industries)

**Character Limits:**
- Post: 3,000 chars
- "See more" cut: ~140 chars (mobile), ~210 chars (desktop)

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first 140 characters
- Liberal use of line breaks
- 3-5 hashtags
- End with question to drive engagement

**Prohibited:**
- "Tenho o prazer de anunciar..."
- "Estou muito feliz em compartilhar..."
- Dense text blocks

### Instagram (Visual Industries)

**Character Limits:**
- Caption: 2,200 chars
- "More" cut: ~125 chars

**Best Practices:**
- Carousel posts (5-10 slides) for education
- One point per slide
- 5-7 hashtags
- Strategic emoji use (1-3)

### X/Twitter (Tech, Creative)

**Character Limits:**
- Single tweet: 280 chars
- Thread: 4-8 tweets

**Best Practices:**
- Lead with strongest point
- 1-2 hashtags max
- Threads for detailed content
- Conversational tone

## Language Patterns

### Portuguese (Brazilian)

**Formal indicators:**
- "Você" (not "tu" except in South)
- Professional but warm
- Avoid excessive formality

**Section titles:**
- "Sobre nós" / "Quem somos"
- "Serviços" / "Áreas de atuação"
- "Contato" / "Fale conosco"
- "Blog" / "Conteúdo"

**CTA patterns:**
- "Me chama no WhatsApp"
- "Fala comigo"
- "Estou à disposição"
- "Podemos conversar"

### English

**Section titles:**
- "About" / "About Us"
- "Services" / "What We Do"
- "Contact" / "Get in Touch"
- "Insights" / "Blog"

**CTA patterns:**
- "Let's discuss"
- "Schedule a consultation"
- "Get in touch"
- "Learn more"
